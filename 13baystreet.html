Property Overview

This bright and airy apartment, in the landmark Chapelfield building, offers a centrally located position close to transport links and overlooks the bustling suburb of Glebe.

With subtle nods to it's heritage, the apartment offers hints of the buildings brick facade in the interior walls too.

Simply finished with exceptional storage space and an oversized walk-in wardrobe.
