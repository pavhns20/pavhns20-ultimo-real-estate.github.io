STANLEY'S HOUSE

49 Baxter Road, Alexandria, NSW 2015

Property Description

A Victorian warehouse conversion is nothing short of brilliant. Located in Baxter Road and designed as a collaboration between Aisling Crawford, Belgian architect Victor Van Duysen and 7b Architects, this modern home offers maximum storage and industrial accents combined with a neutral color scheme.

The kitchen island is decorated with Biani marble and the living area features a full width bi-fold glass doors behind which lead to the secluded garden. The subtle use of pink lighting adds a refreshing touch to this striking warehouse conversion.Fully air-conditioned with underfloor heating.
