Ultimo Suburb Profile

What It Offers

Essentially an extended corner of the Sydney CBD, Ultimo differs slightly from absolute-central Sydney in its disproportionately high percentage of students which call it a temporary home. Ultra-convenient given its proximity to everything else within walking distance nearby, the suburb offers easy access to everything Sydney has to offer

Although the associated high prices, crowds and noise make it a definite choice for those who don’t need too much space or greenery to be satisfied, there are many aspects of this vibrant suburb that make it an excellent choice for those wishing to be central to transport links, access to the city and all it has to offer.
